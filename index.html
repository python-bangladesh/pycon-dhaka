PyCon Dhaka 2016

15th October, Saturday (10 AM to 6 PM)

PyCon Dhaka is an annual conference aimed at promoting and educating people about the Python programming language. PyCharmers (Python Bangladesh) User Group is proud to present the second Python conference in Dhaka.

It will be bigger and better this time with lots of surprising and attractive things.
